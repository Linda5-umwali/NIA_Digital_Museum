06 — About NIA

Our Manifesto

NIA means purpose. We built this museum with a single one: to give every artist a world-class stage — and every curious human a portal to discovery.

The museum of the future is alive, participatory, and infinite. It is not a building; it is a relationship between makers and witnesses, between creators and collectors, between the present moment and all that endures.
